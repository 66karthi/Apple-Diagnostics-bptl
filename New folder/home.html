In apple dtiagnostics we may include the study of different clinical samples, such as blood, urine, throat washings… etc,
Apple diagnosis is based on isolation of the virus by cell culture, electron microscopy, serological assay, and nucleic acid detectionbased techniques (RT-PCR).
With regard to serological tests, ELISA, antigen-detection assays and serum neutralisation tests can be used.
An immunohistochemical assay in formalin-fixed skin biopsy samples to detect Ebola virus has been recently developed.
